Lawless Language Arts is an academic coaching service with more than 15 years of experience leading students to better grades, better test scores, and better future opportunities. Our customers are located in the metropolitan Detroit area and across the country; students’ ages range from pre-K to adults. The focus of our tutoring is generally English and Language Arts, but we offer expertise in ACT and SAT preparation in the English, Reading, and Science sections of the tests, college entry assistance, English as a Second Language, teaching early reading and writing, and business and career communications. Our students have been successful in increasing school grades, developing language skills, raising ACT and SAT scores, gaining college acceptance to preferred schools, and winning big scholarships and writing awards. We work one-on-one with students and focus on areas that need improvement. We always go the extra mile for achievement and encourage students to build their strengths. In the process, we have fun and enjoy learning. Students enhance their abilities to express and present their ideas clearly. Ms. Janet Lawless, M.A.,. is the director. She has over twenty years of teaching experience, teaches college English and private school English classes, and is a published author of both news articles and creative work. Her experience has led to her be proficient in K-12 and college ELA curriculum, and ACT, SAT, and other standardized tests. She has a passion for teaching and students, and the growth of her tutoring services and the success of her students is one of her proudest accomplishments.
